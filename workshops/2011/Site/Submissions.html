LFMTP 2011

6th International Workshop on

Logical Frameworks and Meta-languages:

Theory and Practice

Nijmegen, The Netherlands - August 27 26, 2011

 
 

The submission and review process involves four steps.

  1. 1.Authors initiate the process by submitting a title and an abstract. This must be done electronically and through the EasyChair server for the workshop. This server is accessible through the URL https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=lfmtp2011.

  2. 2.Authors later complement the title and abstract with the actual paper that they would like to have considered for presentation at the workshop and for publication in its proceedings. These papers can be of different kinds; e.g. they could be system descriptions, short accounts of work in progress, or detailed, technical presentations of new results. All submissions must be in PDF form prepared using the EPTCS macro package in LaTeX and must not be more than 15 pages in length.

  3. 3.Submitted papers will be reviewed by the Program Committee to decide appropriateness for presentation at the workshop. The nature of the submission will be taken into account in determining acceptance and in allotting time for presentation.

  4. 4.Authors of accepted papers must prepare a final version that takes into account any comments provided by their reviewers. They must submit these revised versions again through the EasyChair server and must present their papers at the workshop.


The accepted papers will be distributed to the workshop participants in an informal proceedings. A formal version of the proceedings will be made available after the workshop presentations through the Electronic Proceedings in Theoretical Computer Science (EPTCS) series.


The deadlines for the different phases:

  1. -Abstract submission: May 16, 2011

  2. -Paper submission: May 23, 2011

  3. -Notification of acceptance: June 22, 2011

  4. -Submission of revised versions: August 1, 2011


The presentations at the workshop will take place on August 27 26, 2011.
